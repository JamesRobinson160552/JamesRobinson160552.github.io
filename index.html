Hi! I'm James. I am in my fourth and final year of a Bachelor of Computer Science degree from Acadia University and loving it! Outside of programming, I also enjoy taking care of my many dorm-room plants, collecting vinyl records, and learning to play the guitar - and gaming of course! Skills

I have experience programming in Python, Java, C, C++, C#, website building and maintenance with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, database management with SQL and NOSQL based tools, and machine learning tools in Python like TensorFlow, PyTorch, Keras, and Pandas.
